A. Bi




Alice Bi is an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park double-majoring in English and International Relations. She's lived in Taipei, Beijing, Singapore, and now College Park - where she's a member of the Jiménez-Porter Writers' House. She's interested in the digital and the liminal.
