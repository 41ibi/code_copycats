J. Nusch




Carlos J. Nusch He is a Professor and Bachelor of Letters from the National University of La Plata and a Master's in Digital Humanities from the Universidad Nacional de Educación a Distancia. He has published several articles collaborative academic work, digital repositories, digitization of cultural heritage, analysis of political discourse and classical, medieval and modern literature. He works at the Service for the Dissemination of Intellectual Creation (SEDICI) of the UNLP, at the Library Link Project (PREBI) and at the CIC-Digital repository (CICPBA).

My goals for this class are:

  • Learn everything I can about Web technologies
  • Learn to use tools for the creation and management of digital editions
  • Develop skills that allow me to create and manage my own editions
  • See how the use of these technologies can facilitate, add value and spread my work
