J. Goldsmidt




Julieta Y. Goldsmidt is a Clinical psychologist and psychoanalyst. Ph.D student in psychology (UBA). Researcher specializing in topology and its uses in the field of mental health. Presenter at multiple research conferences (UBA, AASM, AAHD) as well as co-author of books and publications in academic journals.
